Organisation Structure

The Agency has the following structure:

  • The Headquarters (located in the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja)
  • Four Departments
    • Regional Geology
    • Economic Geology
    • APplied and Engineering Geology
    • Administration and Finance
  • Four National Research Centers
    • National Geosciences Research Laboratory, Kaduna, Kaduna State.
    • National Centre for Marine Geosciences, Yenegoa, Bayelsa State
    • National Geohazard Monitoring Centre, Awka, Anambra State.
    • National Geosciences Information Centre, FCT, Abuja
